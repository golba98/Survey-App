About This Survey

This is an anonymous survey for a university data visualisation project. We are collecting responses from South African students and young adults about cost of living, work pressure, and daily economic pressures.

Your responses will only be used in grouped, anonymous form for data analysis. No personally identifying information is collected.

Estimated time: 3-5 minutes

Privacy Notice
